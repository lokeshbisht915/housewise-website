How it Works

Step 01

The House owner contacts Housewise and enters into an agreement with Housewise to manage/rent-out the property (Flat/Bungalow) on the house owners behalf.

Step 02

House owner hands over keys and relevant property documents to the Housewise operations team assigned to the property.

Step 03

The Housewise operations team searches for tenants with a good background.

Step 04

On finalizing the prospective tenant, Housewise does the following;

a) Background check of the tenant.

b) Obtain the owners approval with evidence of background check.

Step 05

After successful verification of the prospective tenant, Housewise prepares the rental agreement/online or offline to be signed by both parties - the house owner and the tenant.

Step 06

a) The tenant transfers the deposit and monthly rent directly to the house owners account.

b) Housewise continues to maintain the property until the end of the agreement.
